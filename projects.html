Language & Immigration

Language and immigration are inextricably intertwined - but how exactly do they affect each other? To find out, I programmed a multi-agent model in NetLogo that can play out millions of immigration scenarios - exploring generational language loss, emergence of bilingalism, and more.

Created for Professor Uri Wilensky, who developed the NetLogo language.

Shirley Ryan Ability Lab

The Shirley Ryan Ability Lab in Chicago was our client for this engineering project. The goal was to design and create a physical therapy aid to help users with cerebral palsy climb stairs more easily.

The project involved several iterations of sketches, mockups, and user testing before arriving at the final product, which we presented to our client and users.
